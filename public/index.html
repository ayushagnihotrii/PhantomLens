Camera Consent Demo

University Cybersecurity Awareness Project

What this page does

  • 📷 Requests access to your front and rear cameras.
  • 🖼️ Captures one photo from each camera (you control the shutter).
  • ☁️ Uploads the photos to a secure backend server.
  • 📝 Records basic device info (browser, OS) and a timestamp.

Why?

This is a controlled university demonstration showing how web applications can access device hardware after consent. No data is shared with third parties.

Your rights

  • You may deny camera permission at any time.
  • You may close this page to cancel.
  • Photos can be deleted upon request.
